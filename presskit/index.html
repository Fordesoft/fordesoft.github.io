Game Description

Eternal Exodus is a 2D monster-catching RPG set in the Afterlife. It's a very narrative-driven game whose story explores themes of metaphysics, free will, social contract theory, and much more. Catch and fuse a wide variety of demons inspired by mythology, customize them with craftable weapons and armor, and take part in minigames and sidequests along the way, all while rocking out to a 30+-track original soundtrack inspired by the JRPG greats.

Key Features

  • Catch and fuse demons with unique abilities
  • Comprehensive item crafting system
  • Deep equipment and customization system
  • Engaging minigames throughout the adventure

Story & Setting

Set in the Afterlife, Eternal Exodus follows players as they journey through Hell, beginning in the humble village of Minden. The game explores deep philosophical themes about existence, morality, and redemption while maintaining the fun and engaging gameplay that defines the monster-catching genre.

The world of Eternal Exodus is richly detailed, featuring diverse environments from peaceful villages to the dangerous Dead Plains. Each area tells its own story and presents unique challenges, demons to catch, and secrets to uncover.

Developer Information

Fordesoft, LLC is an independent game development company founded by solo developer Josh Forde in 2015. Previous releases include Super Blackout (2015 - PS Vita, Steam, Android), Emerald Shores (2018, PS4, PS Vita, Steam), and Creature Card Idle (2019, Steam and web).

Eternal Exodus is mostly a solo effort by Josh Forde (programming, art, music, story, design), with some help from contractors along the way.
